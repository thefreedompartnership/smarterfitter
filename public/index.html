Here's a list of the tools we've created so far:

  • Food/Calorie Database - search our database of over 7,000 foods and find out nutrient values for common serving sizes
  • RunLog - log your runs and walks by clicking around the route on a map
  • Body Mass Index (BMI) Calculator - determine whether your body fat level is too low, too high, or just right
